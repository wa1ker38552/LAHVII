VERBAIIZE
A hands-free assistant powered by Google text-to-speech utilizing chat GPT to assist you
ABOUT
We wanted to create an assistant that could help you with searching information accurately and doing basic tasks. However we realized that the current assistants on the market could not provide this service. GPT is vastly better than answering basic questions than Google's built-in assistant is and we used this to our advantage. Compared to a Google Home, our assistant gives clearer and more concise answers.
MISSION
Our main goal to improve and expand on a different aspects of virtual assistants. Assistants such as Google Home can often be reliable and redirect you to websites instead of speaking the information back to you. Additionally, they can only answer responses to simple questions or take commands, and cannot understand more complex questions. The new chatbot Chat GPT is much stronger at understanding and formulating complex ideas and solutions. By integrating this software to a text to speech function, people are able to talk to virtual assistants similar to talking to an intelligent human being.
